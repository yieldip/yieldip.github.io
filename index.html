7 Reasons to Register a Trade Mark

Registering a trade mark provides exclusive legal rights to use and protect your brand, offers perpetual renewable protection and commercialization opportunities, and serves as a valuable business asset that builds brand identity and generates revenue.

Trade Mark Filing Strategy: Word Mark, Logo Mark or Composite Mark?

For optimal brand protection, register three separate trade marks — word mark, logo mark, and composite mark — or at minimum the word and logo marks separately to ensure flexible usage.

Be Your Own Dentist - Yay or Nay?

While filing a trademark application online through IPOS is straightforward, engaging an IP professional for pre-filing conflict searches and strategic advice remains crucial to avoid costly objections and oppositions.
